# Partner Channel (API)

## What it is
A program offered by some platforms (like Hostaway) for software companies that need to integrate with many accounts. Partners get their own credentials and a dedicated integration path, rather than sharing individual account API keys. It's designed for vendors (pricing tools, channel managers), not for one-off consulting arrangements.

## Tourism Translation
**"Like a hotel chain's vendor portal — approved suppliers get their own login badge instead of borrowing the GM's keycard."**

Imagine Marriott has 500 hotels. A linen supplier that services 50 of those hotels doesn't get 50 separate GM keycards. Instead, Marriott enrolls them as an approved vendor with their own portal access scoped to the hotels they serve. That's a Partner Channel. It's different from a one-time contractor who just needs to check one hotel's inventory — for them, a simpler, more limited approach (like a proxy) makes more sense.
