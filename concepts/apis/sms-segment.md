# SMS Segment

## What it is
The unit SMS carriers bill by. A plain-text message fits up to 160 characters in ONE segment. Anything longer is split into multiple segments behind the scenes. The guest sees one message, but you pay per segment — and providers count segments, not messages.

## Tourism Translation
**A tow vehicle with a weight limit.** Your boat fits on one trailer if it's under 25 feet. Add a dinghy on top? Now you need a second trailer. The marina doesn't charge you by "trip," it charges you per trailer hauled. Every SMS segment costs you again, even though the guest reads what looks like a single text.
