# MMS (Multimedia Messaging Service)

## What it is
The technology that lets you send photos, videos, and audio via text message. Regular SMS is text-only (up to 160 characters). MMS extends texting to include media files. Most modern phones support MMS, but not all software APIs do.

## Tourism Translation
**A guest texting you a photo of their ID for check-in verification.** When a guest sends a picture of their driver's license or healthcare badge via text, that's MMS — not regular SMS. Your phone shows it fine, but your property management system might not be able to pull that image in automatically if its API only supports text messages. The photo exists, but it's trapped in the texting app.
