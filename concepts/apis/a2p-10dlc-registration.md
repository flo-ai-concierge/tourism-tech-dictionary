# A2P 10DLC

## What it is
Application-to-Person messaging over a 10-Digit Long Code. US carrier rules that require businesses to *register* any regular phone number (like +1-667-270-8226) that sends automated SMS. Without registration, traffic gets throttled, blocked, or charged surcharge rates. The registration tells carriers "this number is a legitimate business, not a spammer."

## Tourism Translation
**Business license for a food truck.** You can park your pickup anywhere and hand a friend a sandwich — that's personal use. But the moment you start selling food commercially, the city requires you to register, carry insurance, and operate from approved stops. A2P 10DLC is the FCC saying "you're now a business texting your guest list at scale — register your number with us or we'll shut you down at the curb."
