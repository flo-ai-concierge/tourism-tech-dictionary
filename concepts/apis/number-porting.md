# Number Porting

## What it is
Moving a phone number from one telecommunications provider to another while keeping the same number. The process is regulated and typically takes 1-2 weeks. The number's functionality transfers completely — calls and texts to that number route to the new provider.

## Tourism Translation
**Switching your property's reservation phone line to a new answering service without changing the number.** Guests, OTAs, and your marketing materials all have your (301) 674-XXXX number. When you switch providers, you port that number so nothing changes for anyone calling in — they still dial the same number, but a different service picks up behind the scenes.
