# Audit Trail (API Logging)

## What it is
Recording every request an external party makes to your system — who asked, what they asked for, when, and what data was returned. This creates an accountability record that you can review if something goes wrong, or to understand usage patterns over time.

## Tourism Translation
**"Like a hotel security camera log — the vendor can come in, but every visit is recorded so you can review what they accessed."**

Your revenue management company pulls reservation data from your system every day. With an audit trail, you have a timestamped log: "April 16, 9:02 AM — OASI requested all reservations for Property 5, March 2026." If they later claim they never saw certain data, or if you suspect they're pulling more than they need, you have the receipts. It's the security camera footage of your data room.
