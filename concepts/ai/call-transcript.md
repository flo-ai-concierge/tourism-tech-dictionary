# Call Transcript (AI-Generated)

## What it is
A text record of a phone conversation automatically generated by an AI system. Includes speaker identification (who said what), timestamps, and the full dialogue. In the FLOHOM context, Sona (OpenPhone's AI assistant) generates these when it handles calls.

## Tourism translation
Like a front desk keeping typed notes of exactly what was discussed when a guest called — who said what, word for word. Instead of a human writing notes from memory, the AI creates a perfect transcript in real time.

## When you'd see it
- AI-handled phone calls (Sona, Google Duplex, etc.)
- Voicemail transcriptions (audio → text)
- Meeting recordings with speaker diarization
- Customer service call logs

## Key fields
- **dialogue**: Array of segments with content, speaker identifier, and timestamps
- **identifier**: Phone number of the speaker (your number = AI/host, other number = guest)
- **userId**: Internal user ID (null for both Sona and external callers in OpenPhone)

## Learned from
Built Sona transcript sync on Apr 16, 2026. Speaker identification uses phone number matching (our FLOHOM numbers = "Sona", guest numbers = "Guest") since userId is null for both.
