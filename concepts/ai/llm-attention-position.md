# LLM Attention Position

## What it is
AI language models pay the most attention to content at the beginning and end of their input — especially the end. This "recency bias" means instructions placed as the last message before the AI generates its response are the hardest to ignore. Important directives buried in the middle of a long prompt often get overlooked.

## Tourism Translation
**The last thing said in a shift briefing.** When a hotel manager briefs the morning team, the last instruction before everyone heads to their stations is what sticks. If the manager says "the VIP in Room 301 must get fresh flowers" right at the end, everyone remembers. If that same instruction was buried in the middle of a 20-minute briefing between housekeeping schedules and maintenance updates, it would likely be forgotten.
