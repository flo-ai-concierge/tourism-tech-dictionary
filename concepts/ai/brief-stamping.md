# Brief Stamping

## What it is
Automatically appending important admin decisions directly into a summary document (like a FLO Brief) the instant they're created, so downstream systems see them immediately without manual refresh. The stamp is marked as highest-priority so the AI treats it as an override.

## Tourism Translation
**Like a hotel manager writing "VIP — comp the minibar" directly on a guest's folio card the instant the decision is made.** Every department — housekeeping, room service, the bar — sees it automatically on the next check without being told separately. No one needs to call around or wait for an email. The note is right there on the card, marked as a management decision that overrides standard policy.

## When you'll encounter it
- When admin notes need to immediately influence AI-generated replies
- When you see "ADMIN DECISION" or "OVERRIDES ALL ABOVE" markers in system context
- When building systems where human decisions must take priority over automated logic
