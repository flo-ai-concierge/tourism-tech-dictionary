# Deferral Detection

## What it is
Detecting when an AI gives a generic "let me check on that" or "I'll get back to you" response instead of actually answering the question — then automatically rerouting to a smarter system that has the real information.

## Tourism Translation
**Catching when your front desk keeps saying "I'll get back to you" instead of answering.**

You hire a new concierge who, when asked about local restaurants, always says "Great question! Let me check with the team and follow up." That's not helpful — the guest wants an answer now. Deferral detection is like a supervisor who monitors the concierge, notices the pattern, and steps in: "Actually, I have the restaurant guide right here" — pulling out the binder and giving the guest real recommendations.

## Why it matters
An AI concierge was consistently deferring on chef and massage questions even though the information existed in the knowledge bank. The system now detects these non-answers and falls back to a different strategy that has access to the full service directory.
