# Comp Set Analysis

## What it is
Comparing your property's pricing and performance metrics (occupancy, ADR, RevPAR) against a curated set of similar competing properties in your market. Revenue managers use comp sets to understand whether they're priced too high, too low, or right in line with the market.

## Tourism Translation
**"Like a hotel GM checking what the Marriott and Hilton across the street are charging tonight before setting their own room rate."**

If your 3-bedroom oceanfront in Virginia Beach is listed at $350/night, but five comparable properties nearby are all at $280, you're probably overpriced and losing bookings. If they're all at $400, you're leaving money on the table. Comp set analysis is the discipline of identifying the right "neighbors" to compare against and adjusting your strategy based on where you stand relative to them.
