# STR Market Data

## What it is
Aggregate performance numbers (ADR, occupancy, revenue, listing counts, seasonality) for all short-term rentals in a specific city or geographic area — not one property, but the whole market.

## Plain English
Instead of asking "how is this one Airbnb doing?" you're asking "how are ALL vacation rentals in Virginia Beach doing as a group?" Market data gives you the benchmark — the average, the trend, the seasonal pattern — so you can compare any individual property or plan against the broader market.

## Tourism Analogy
Like a destination's annual tourism report. The local convention bureau publishes: average hotel rate this summer was $280, occupancy peaked at 87% in July, 412 active properties. That's market data — the snapshot of an entire destination's performance, not any one hotel.

## Key metrics included
- ADR (average nightly rate across all listings)
- Occupancy rate (% of available nights that were booked)
- Revenue per listing per month
- Active listing count by bedroom type
- Seasonality curve (month-by-month demand)
- Supply growth (year-over-year change in listing count)
