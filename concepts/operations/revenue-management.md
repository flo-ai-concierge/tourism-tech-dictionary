# Revenue Management (RevM)

## What it is
The discipline of setting optimal prices across properties and time periods to maximize total revenue. It combines demand forecasting, comp set analysis, channel strategy, length-of-stay restrictions, and dynamic pricing rules. In short-term rentals, it's often outsourced to specialized firms who adjust rates daily.

## Tourism Translation
**"Like an airline adjusting ticket prices daily — same seat costs $200 on Tuesday and $450 on Friday because demand is different."**

A revenue manager for FLOHOM looks at all 15 properties every day and asks: "Should this property be $250 tonight or $320? Should we set a 3-night minimum for this weekend? Should we drop the rate on this slow Tuesday to grab a last-minute booking?" They use market data, competitor rates, historical patterns, and booking velocity to make these calls. The goal isn't just to fill every night — it's to fill every night at the highest price the market will pay.
