# API Credits vs. Subscription Billing

## What it is
Two separate billing rails that live on the same platform account. Your monthly subscription covers one set of uses (usually humans acting in the app). A prepaid credit balance funds a different set — typically anything triggered by a program instead of a person. When credits hit zero, that path fails silently, even though your subscription is fully paid. Most telecom and SMS providers split their billing this way (Quo, Twilio, OpenPhone, etc.).

## Tourism Translation
**All-inclusive resort pass vs. the pool-bar tab.** Your room booking covers meals at the restaurant and drinks at the main bar. But cocktails delivered poolside come out of a separate tab you have to keep funded with your credit card. If that tab hits zero, the poolside bartender shakes his head — even though your room is paid through Sunday. Same hotel, same guest, two totally different billing lanes. Don't assume "I paid for the resort" means "everything is covered."
