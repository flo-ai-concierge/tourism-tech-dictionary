# Automation Credits

## What it is
A third, AI-specific billing bucket some platforms carve out (Quo calls theirs "Sona credits"). Separate from both the base subscription AND pay-as-you-go SMS/call credits. These fund features like voicemail transcription, smart call routing, AI summaries, and third-party AI integrations. When depleted, AI features pause but regular voice and text keep working.

## Tourism Translation
**The concierge's experience budget.** Your all-inclusive covers rooms and food. Cocktails come from a separate tab. And the concierge's ability to arrange private chefs, drivers, or surprise excursions comes out of a *third* monthly budget the GM sets. Run through that budget and the concierge stops booking extras — but the hotel still runs, rooms still get cleaned, meals still get served. Three budgets, three failure modes, and "I paid my bill" doesn't tell you which bucket is actually empty.
